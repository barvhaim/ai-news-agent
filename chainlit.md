# AI-News Assistant

Welcome! I'm your AI-powered assistant for staying up-to-date with the latest in AI and machine learning.

## What I Can Do

I can help you discover and explore AI/ML content from multiple sources:

- **Hugging Face Papers** - Daily trending research papers with AI-generated summaries, keywords, and community engagement metrics
- **Hugging Face Spaces** - Interactive demos and applications from the community, sorted by trending score
- **Hacker News Stories** - Latest AI/ML tech news and discussions filtered from Hacker News top stories
- **arXiv Papers** - Search or browse recent AI research from arXiv's Artificial Intelligence category (cs.AI)

## How to Use

Simply ask me questions like:
- "What are the top 5 trending AI papers today?"
- "Show me the most popular Hugging Face Spaces"
- "What are the latest AI stories on Hacker News?"
- "Find recent arXiv papers about neural networks"
- "Search arXiv for papers on large language models"
- "Show me recent computer vision papers and demos"

I use advanced reasoning to understand your questions and fetch the most relevant information from multiple AI/ML news sources.

## Powered By

- **BeeAI Framework** - ReActAgent architecture for intelligent task planning
- **Hugging Face** - Daily papers and trending spaces data
- **Hacker News API** - Top tech stories with AI/ML filtering
- **arXiv API** - Academic AI research papers (cs.AI category)
